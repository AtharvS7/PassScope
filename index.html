    Password Strength Formula

    Scoring Rules:
    • Length ≥ 12 characters → +20 points
    • Length ≥ 8 characters → +10 points
    • Uppercase letters → +10
    • Lowercase letters → +10
    • Numbers → +10
    • Special characters → +10
    Penalties:
    • Repeated characters → -10
    • Common passwords → -15
    • Dictionary words → -10
    • Low character uniqueness → -10
    Final Formula:

    Final Score = Bonuses − Penalties

    Score Range: 0 – 100
